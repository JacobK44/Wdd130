    HISTORY

    White Water Rafting was founded in 2010 with one goal in mind: to share our passion for adventure and beauty of the great outdoors with everyone. It all started with a small group of friends who spent their weekends exploring the ripids of Nile River, dreaming of a way to turn their love for rafting into something bigger. With just two rafts and a whole lot of enthusiasm, we launched our first guided rafting tours.
